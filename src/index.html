FurTones

Built by Max, Mayukhi and Oli for AstonHack25.

How to play
1. Click on the stave to draw in notes.
2. Press the 'Play' button to send them to the keyboard and watch your creations play out!
3. Press the 'Pause' button to stop the keyboard from playing.
4. Press the 'Clear' button to remove all the notes from the stave so you can start again!
